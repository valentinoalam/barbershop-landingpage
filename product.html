Precision Styling Gel

Description

Our Precision Styling Gel is designed for the modern man who demands control and precision in his grooming routine. This high-performance gel provides a strong, flexible hold that lasts all day, allowing you to sculpt and shape your hair with ease. Whether you're aiming for a sleek, polished look or a textured, casual style, our gel delivers exceptional results without stiffness or flaking. Infused with nourishing ingredients, it also helps to keep your hair healthy and hydrated, ensuring a natural, well-groomed appearance.

Ingredients

Water, PVP, VP/VA Copolymer, Glycerin, Carbomer, Triethanolamine, Polysorbate 20, Fragrance, Disodium EDTA, Phenoxyethanol, Ethylhexylglycerin, Aloe Barbadensis Leaf Juice, Tocopheryl Acetate (Vitamin E), Panthenol (Pro-Vitamin B5).
